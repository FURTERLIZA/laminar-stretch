laminar stretch slices audio into short overlapping grains and rebuilds it at a slower rate — time stretched, pitch unchanged. layering grains at different sizes and offsets is where the texture comes from.

if you make something with it, share it.

youtube bluesky github

privacy

laminar stretch runs entirely in your browser. your audio is never uploaded — all processing happens locally on your device. no data is collected.
